FQS



Q: I need doughnuts and coffee for a meeting tomorrow! What can I do?

A: Call the café most convenient for pick up and the baristas will get it set up for you.



Q: How much are your doughnuts?

A: Our doughnuts are best priced by the dozen at $23.49 each!



Q: Do you sell coffee-to-go totes?

A: We sure do! Coffee for 12 (plus cups, cream and sugar) is $19.95 and available at all Top Pot cafés.



Q: I need a copy of my receipt, how do I get one?

A: Send us an email with the date of purchase,

café you purchased from and the last 4 digits of the card you used. Simple as that!



Q: What type of oil are your doughnuts fried in?

A: We use sustainably harvested zero trans-fat palm oil.



Q: What are the common allergens in your doughnuts?

A: Wheat, Soy, Dairy (milk products), Corn and Eggs.



Q: Can I buy a gift card online?

A: Not at this time. Please send us an email with a phone number and a good time to call and we can get one in the mail for you!


Q: Do you ship out of state?

A: We do not currently offer shipping.
